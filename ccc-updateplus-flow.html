Repair Status Notification Flow

All customer-facing messages sent throughout the collision repair lifecycle. Time-based messages auto-fire based on the percentage of time elapsed toward the promise date entered in CCC ONE. All other messages are triggered by shop actions or delivered automatically post-delivery.

Trigger:
Shop-triggered Shop takes action in CCC ONE
Time-based Auto-sent at % of promise date elapsed
Use-case driven Timing depends on intake workflow
Post-delivery Auto-sent after vehicle-out
Repair Status Messages Active repair lifecycle · Messages 1–10
1
Welcome (Initial Message) Use-case driven
Introduces the customer to UpdatePlus and sets expectations for repair communications. Send timing varies depending on which intake workflow the shop uses.
Online appointment  →  sent at time of scheduling
Photo estimate  →  sent at estimate submission
Standard RO  →  sent when vehicle is dropped off
SMS Message
Status: [Customer First Name], [Repair Facility Name] will be sending you repair status updates and a survey. Msg/data rates may apply. Reply "STOP" to cancel.
Trigger: Varies by workflow — appointment, photo estimate, or vehicle-in event
2
Vehicle-In (Dropped Off) Shop-triggered
Confirms the vehicle has been received at the facility and repair work is beginning. Provides reassurance and sets the repair in motion for the customer.

Shop-triggered events
8
Repair Completed Shop-triggered
Notifies the customer that repairs are finished and the vehicle is ready for pickup. Typically the most anticipated message in the entire sequence.
SMS Message
Update: Your [Vehicle Make] is all fixed and ready to come home! We will call you soon if we haven't already, to arrange pick up. [Repair Facility Name]. Reply "STOP" to cancel.
Trigger: Shop marks repair as complete in CCC ONE
9
Vehicle Out Shop-triggered
Confirms the vehicle has been returned to the customer. Closes the active repair loop and initiates the post-delivery feedback sequence.
SMS Message
Update: [Customer First Name], confirming that you picked up your vehicle today. We enjoyed working with you. [Repair Facility Name]. Reply "STOP" to cancel.
Trigger: Shop marks vehicle as delivered / out in CCC ONE
DATE
Promise Date Update  (conditional — sent as needed) Shop-triggered
Sent whenever the shop changes the estimated completion date in CCC ONE. Critical for proactive expectation management — prevents customer frustration from unannounced schedule changes.
SMS Message
Update: Hi [Customer First Name]. We have updated your estimated completion date from [oldPromiseDate as MM/DD/YYYY] to [newPromiseDate as MM/DD/YYYY]. [Repair Facility Name]. Reply "STOP" to cancel. [Carwise URL]
Trigger: Shop modifies the promise / delivery date in CCC ONE  ·  ⚠ Recalculates all time-based message windows
Post-Delivery & Feedback Auto-sent after vehicle-out · Messages 11–13
11
Post Delivery Feedback Post-delivery
General repair experience survey capturing overall customer satisfaction. Provides the shop with actionable quality feedback across all repair orders.
SMS Message
Thank you for trusting [Repair Facility Name] to repair your [Vehicle Make]. Please answer one question about your satisfaction. [Survey Link]
Trigger: Auto-sent after vehicle-out event (delayed send) · All ROs
12
Insurance CSI Post-delivery
Customer Satisfaction Index survey focused on the insurance claim experience. Results feed directly into insurer scorecards and DRP performance metrics for the shop.
SMS Message
Hi [Customer First Name], [Insurance Company] would like to hear from you about your recent vehicle repair experience at [Repair Facility Name]. [Survey Link] Reply "STOP" to cancel.
Trigger: Auto-sent after vehicle-out · Insurance-related ROs only
13
Shop CSI Post-delivery
Shop-specific satisfaction survey measuring repair quality, communication, and overall experience. Helps shops benchmark performance and identify areas for improvement.
SMS Message
Hi [Customer First Name], [Repair Facility Name] would like to hear from you about your recent vehicle repair experience. [Survey Link] Reply "STOP" to cancel.
Trigger: Auto-sent after vehicle-out · All ROs
