Your 3-Step Decision Path

Get the facts before you decide — it only takes a few minutes

1

Get a Free Estimate from a Local Shop

Use Carwise to find a trusted body shop near you and request a repair estimate. Many shops offer free written estimates — no commitment required. You can even submit photos online for a preliminary quote.

→ Find shops that offer free estimates
2

Compare the Estimate to Your Deductible

Once you have the repair cost, compare it to your insurance deductible. If the repair is close to or less than your deductible, paying out of pocket may make more sense — filing a claim could raise your premium without much benefit.

💡 General rule: If repairs cost less than 2× your deductible, consider paying out of pocket.
3

Decide — Then Act With Confidence

With a real estimate in hand, you're in control. Pay the shop directly if it makes sense, or contact your insurer to open a claim knowing exactly what you're dealing with. Either way, Carwise helps you find the right shop for the job.
