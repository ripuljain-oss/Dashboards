How to Join

Activating Carwise
Takes Minutes

The only requirement is that you're an active CCC ONE customer. If you are, here's exactly how to get your shop listed — no IT team needed.

⚙
Prerequisite
You Must Be a CCC ONE Customer

Carwise is exclusively available to shops that actively use CCC ONE as their shop management system. If you're not yet a CCC ONE customer, you'll need to sign up with CCC first — see the panel on the right for more info.

Already using CCC ONE? Great — keep reading. You're one setting away from going live.
1
You Do This
Log Into Your CCC ONE Account

Open CCC ONE and navigate to your shop settings. You can access this from the main dashboard using your existing username and password — no new account required.

2
You Do This
Find the Carwise Setting

In your CCC ONE settings, navigate to Shop Profile → Carwise Listing. You'll see a toggle that controls whether your shop appears on Carwise. It may be labeled "Carwise Profile" or "Consumer Listing Visibility" depending on your CCC ONE version.

Can't find it? Contact CCC ONE support at 1-800-831-0027 and ask them to walk you through enabling your Carwise listing.
3
You Do This
Toggle It On & Review Your Profile

Enable the Carwise listing toggle. You'll be shown a preview of how your shop profile will appear on Carwise — pulled from your existing CCC ONE data. Review it for accuracy: shop name, address, phone number, hours, and certifications.

If any information looks incorrect, update it in your main CCC ONE shop profile — changes will automatically sync to your Carwise listing.
✓
Automatic
You're Live on Carwise

Your shop listing goes live on Carwise within 24 hours. From this point on, your profile stays automatically in sync with CCC ONE — and review collection begins automatically once you complete repair orders.

Verified Reviews

How the Review
System Works

Reviews are the most powerful part of your Carwise listing — and unlike any other platform, every review on Carwise is guaranteed to be from a real customer of yours.

🔧
Repair is completed in CCC ONE

When you mark a repair job as complete in your CCC ONE system, this automatically triggers the review process. No extra step required from you.

📱
Customer receives a review invitation

The vehicle owner gets an automated email or text inviting them to share their experience on Carwise. Only the actual customer from that job record receives an invitation — no one else can leave a review for your shop.

⭐
Review appears on your Carwise listing

Once submitted, the review is published on your Carwise profile with a "Verified Repair Customer" badge. Drivers looking at your listing can see that every review comes from a confirmed, real customer — making your ratings more trustworthy than anything on Yelp or Google.

📊
Your rating builds over time automatically

Every job you complete is a potential new review. Your Carwise rating grows organically with every repair — no manual effort, no chasing customers for feedback on multiple platforms.

Common Questions

Questions Shop
Owners Ask Us

Does my shop have to pay anything to be on Carwise? ⌄
No. If you're an active CCC ONE customer, activating your Carwise listing is included at no additional cost. Carwise is a built-in benefit of the CCC ONE platform — not a separate subscription or add-on fee.
What if I'm not a CCC ONE customer? ⌄
Carwise is currently available exclusively to shops that use CCC ONE as their shop management platform. If you're not yet a CCC ONE customer, you'd need to sign up with CCC first. Contact CCC directly at cccis.com to learn about CCC ONE and how to get started. Once you're an active CCC ONE user, enabling Carwise takes just a few clicks.
Can I control what information appears on my listing? ⌄
Your Carwise listing is automatically populated from your CCC ONE account data — so to update any information, you update it within CCC ONE and the change syncs to Carwise automatically. You cannot manually edit your Carwise listing directly, which is by design: this ensures that only accurate, system-verified information appears on your profile.
Can I respond to reviews customers leave? ⌄
Yes. Shop owners can respond to reviews on their Carwise listing. Responding to reviews — especially mixed ones — is a great way to show potential customers that you're attentive and care about quality. You can access your reviews and manage responses through the Carwise shop portal, accessible via your CCC ONE account.
How long does it take to go live after I enable Carwise in CCC ONE? ⌄
Your listing typically goes live on Carwise within 24 hours of enabling the feature in CCC ONE. In many cases it's faster than that. Once live, your shop will appear in search results for drivers in your area — and review collection begins automatically with your next completed repair.
Will my insurance carrier relationships show up on my listing? ⌄
Yes — if you have DRP or preferred shop relationships with insurance carriers that are registered in CCC ONE, those relationships will be reflected on your Carwise listing. When a driver searches Carwise and selects their insurance carrier, your shop will be highlighted as a preferred option for that carrier — giving you a visibility advantage for their policyholders.
Can I turn off my Carwise listing if I want to pause? ⌄
Yes. You can disable your Carwise listing at any time by toggling it off in your CCC ONE settings. Your profile and all reviews are preserved — so if you turn it back on later, your history and ratings will still be there.
