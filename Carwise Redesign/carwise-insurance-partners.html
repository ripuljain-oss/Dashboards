Platform Overview

What is Carwise?

Carwise is a consumer-facing shop-finder and review platform built by CCC Intelligent Solutions — designed to help drivers confidently choose a trusted auto body repair shop after a collision.

🔍
A Trusted Shop Discovery Platform

Carwise helps millions of drivers find, compare, and contact auto body shops near them. It is not a general business directory — every shop on Carwise is a verified collision repair professional operating within the CCC ecosystem.

🛡️
Built on Verified Data — Not Self-Reported Listings

Unlike Yelp or Google, shops do not create their own profiles or write their own descriptions. Every data point — from shop name and hours to certifications and capacity — is pulled directly from the shop's CCC ONE management system.

🤝
Your Network, Surfaced to Consumers

For insurance carriers, Carwise is a powerful channel to surface your preferred and direct-repair program (DRP) shops to policyholders at exactly the moment they need one — right after an accident, before they call a competitor's shop.

📋
A Natural Extension of the Claims Journey

Carwise sits at the intersection of the consumer and the shop — surfacing the right shop, at the right moment, with the right information. When integrated with your carrier workflow, it can accelerate claim initiation and reduce friction for both adjusters and policyholders.

Shop Eligibility & Appearance

Which Shops Appear
on Carwise?

Carwise is not an open directory. Shops appear only through a deliberate, CCC ONE-enabled opt-in process — ensuring every listing represents a real, active collision repair business.

1
Prerequisite
Must Be an Active CCC ONE Customer

Only body shops actively using CCC ONE as their management platform are eligible to appear on Carwise. This ensures every shop has a real, verified operational footprint — not just a listing someone created.

2
Opt-In Required
Shop Chooses to Enable Carwise

Within CCC ONE, the shop actively enables their Carwise listing. This is a deliberate choice — shops are not automatically listed. Enabling the feature gives Carwise permission to publish and sync their profile data.

3
Automated
Profile Populates from CCC ONE Data

Once enabled, the shop's Carwise listing is automatically populated with data directly from their CCC ONE system: shop name, address, hours of operation, certifications (OEM, I-CAR, etc.), insurance relationships, and contact details.

4
Ongoing
Reviews Collected After Verified Repairs

After a repair is completed and logged in CCC ONE, Carwise automatically sends a review request to the vehicle owner. Only customers with completed repairs in the system can submit reviews — eliminating fake or unverified feedback.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can any body shop create a Carwise listing? ⌄
No. A shop must be an active CCC ONE customer and must explicitly enable the Carwise integration within their CCC ONE settings. Carwise is not an open public directory — it is a curated network of CCC ONE-verified shops that have opted in to be listed.
Where does the shop profile data come from? ⌄
All shop profile data — name, address, phone number, hours of operation, certifications, and insurance relationships — is pulled directly from the shop's active CCC ONE account. Shops do not manually populate their own Carwise profiles. Data syncs automatically, so it remains current as the shop updates their own CCC ONE system.
How are reviews collected and verified? ⌄
Review invitations are triggered automatically when a repair is marked complete in CCC ONE. Only the vehicle owner associated with that completed repair job receives a review invitation. Anonymous or unsolicited public reviews are not accepted. This means every review on Carwise is tied to a real, completed repair — eliminating fake reviews and competitive manipulation.
How can our preferred shops be highlighted to our policyholders on Carwise? ⌄
Through a formal carrier partnership with Carwise, your DRP or preferred network shops can be badged and prioritized in consumer search results when a policyholder selects your carrier. This allows policyholders who identify themselves as your customers to immediately see which shops you work with — matching your network to consumer intent at the exact moment of shop selection.
Can Carwise be embedded in our claims portal or mobile app? ⌄
Yes. Carwise offers integration options that allow carriers to surface shop-search functionality directly within their own claims experience — through APIs, embedded widgets, or co-branded landing pages. This allows you to keep your policyholders in your ecosystem while giving them access to the Carwise shop network.
Is Carwise available nationwide? ⌄
Yes. Carwise has coverage across all 50 US states, with shops in virtually every major metro area and most secondary markets. The network reflects the national footprint of CCC ONE-enabled shops that have opted in — a coverage area that continues to grow as more shops activate the Carwise integration.
